Privacy Policy for MFS Inspector

Effective Date: April 29, 2025

App Name: MFS Inspector

Developer: KCI Technologies Inc.

Website: https://kcigeospatial.github.io/mfs-inspector-privacy

1. Introduction

KCI Technologies Inc. (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the MFS Inspector mobile application (“the App”) uses and protects information when you use features that involve device permissions, such as the camera.

2. What Information We Access

Camera

  • The App may request access to the device camera.
  • Purpose: To allow users to capture photos of field conditions, equipment, or test artifacts, and attach them to inspection records.
  • Photos are only stored locally on the device or securely transmitted to authorized KCI project systems.
  • No photos are shared with third parties or used for tracking, biometric analysis, or advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

The App does not collect personal or sensitive user data beyond what is explicitly entered or captured by the user. All data remains under the control of KCI Technologies Inc. and is protected according to organizational security standards. The App does not integrate third-party analytics or advertising libraries.

4. User Consent

By using the App and granting camera permissions, you consent to image capture strictly for documentation purposes as part of KCI’s field reporting workflow.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted at https://kcigeospatial.github.io/mfs-inspector-privacy.
